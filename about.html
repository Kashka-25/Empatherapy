Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Cassidy—the heart behind Empatherapy.

My path has been one of deep feeling, exploration, and transformation. I began in the world of witchcraft and magick, where I learned to listen to the unseen and explore the depths of the human spirit. This opened me to meditation, energy work, and a living relationship with the metaphysical.

Over time, I journeyed into altered states of consciousness and grounded those experiences with studies in holistic counselling and complementary therapies. Along the way, I wove in somatic practices, shadow work, Reiki, and my lifelong love of philosophy, music, and emotions.

I believe emotions are sacred messengers—not here to harm or rule us, but to guide, teach, and sometimes initiate great change. They act as bridges between our human experience and the lives we long to embody. My role is to offer a safe, compassionate space where you can meet those emotions, hear their messages, and uncover the peace and strength within them.

This Isn't About Bypassing Pain

This isn't about bypassing pain or simply talking through it—it's about alchemizing your story into something powerful, compassionate, and deeply you.

I felt like I was an outcast, that nobody understood or cared—forced to do it alone. I sought solace in older people and wise spaces. Knowing how much I suffered, I don't want others to feel alone. I want people to know they don't have to feel like a burden—I offer them a safe space to speak and be heard.

Feeling comes from within—not from the outside. My work is about bringing feeling back into the being: heart, body, mind, and spirit coherence.

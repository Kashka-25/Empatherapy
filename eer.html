EER — Emotional Emergency Room

Support when life asks too much

There are moments when emotion rises like a tide—unexpected, unwielding, and overwhelming. In that space, you don't always have the tools, time, or energy to process it alone. The EER is for the moments when it all feels too much.

Life doesn't pause during overwhelm. Neither do emotions. You don't need a therapy appointment or endless sessions or judgment—it's not therapy, and it's not a fix—it's a pause. A place where you can be held, heard, and seen without needing explanations.

You don't have to hold space for everything alone. Let this soften the weight when the world feels too loud. The EER meets you where you are and gently guides you back to yourself.

Choose the Support You Need in This Moment

EER Pulse — Rapid Relief

$100

When the wave hits suddenly, and you just need someone there.

This is instant support—like a held breath or a moment in time. Nothing prolonged or formalized, but immediate. A brief (30–40 minute) real-time connection via phone or video to help you regulate, ground, and remember you're not alone.

What's Included:

  • Brief (30–40 minute) connection via phone or video
  • Immediate emotional witnessing and attunement
  • Grounding techniques to regulate nervous system
  • Emotional understanding and holding (not therapy)
  • Anchors and stabilization tools to carry with you

EER Deep — Deeper Presence

$125

When you need more room inside to break.

EER Deep is spacious, held, tender. It's for when emotions don't fit neatly in a rush, nor do they leave easily. We stay longer, soften, allow more pause. An hour (up to 70 minutes if needed) to slow into what wants to be felt.

What's Included:

  • Up to 70 minutes to slow into what wants to be felt
  • Deep presence and emotional co-regulation
  • Space to speak/cry/be held via video or voice
  • Ritual and somatic awareness to soothe nervous system
  • Deeply layered listening—no diagnosis, no urgency

How It Works

1. Recognize the Call

When you feel the overwhelming emotional pull—the moment before the breakdown—that's when to reach out.

2. Choose Your Session

EER Pulse for immediate grounding, breath support, and emotional anchoring.
EER Deep for spacious, gentle unfolding and nervous system regulation.

3. Reach Out

Email with subject line "EER Pulse" or "EER Deep" and include:

  • A short description of what you're feeling
  • Whether you prefer phone or video call
  • Your general availability in the next 1–8 hours
  • What your nervous system needs most: to be held, to speak, to breathe

4. Receive Your Session

I'll respond as soon as possible to confirm availability and meet you in the format that suits your nervous system best.

5. Gentle After-Care

After our session, you'll receive:

  • Personalized follow-up anchor—grounding message or reflective prompts
  • Optional follow-up voice message support
  • Space for re-appointments if needed

Please Note

The EER is designed for responsive care, not clinical intervention. While I do my best to hold you in a timely manner, this offering requires a willingness to surrender to the unknown and allow space for spontaneity.

Your request doesn't always guarantee same-day responses, but will be received as soon as I can.

EER — Emotional Emergency Relief

Short-Form, Faster-Response Support

EER exists for moments when emotional support is needed quickly — between full sessions, during a difficult week, or when something arises that can't wait.

These are shorter, more focused sessions offered with increased response priority for subscribers. EER is also included as a 15-minute follow-up with every Empatherapy session, giving you a touchpoint in the weeks after your session when you need it.

EER is not a crisis service or emergency care, but a compassionate bridge during moments of emotional overwhelm.

EER Options

All Empatherapy sessions already include a 15-minute EER follow-up. The subscription and one-off options below are for standalone EER support.

EER Support Subscription

$111 AUD/month

Includes:

  • 2 x 15-minute EER sessions per month
  • Priority response & scheduling
  • Reserved monthly capacity
  • Sessions scheduled within 72 hours of request

Ideal for: Those navigating ongoing instability or transition who want reliable, accessible support between full sessions.

Unused sessions do not roll over, but the presence of support remains.

One-Off EER Session

$75 AUD

15-minute EER session (non-subscriber)

For those who need occasional support outside of a subscription. Availability is limited and prioritized after active subscribers.

  • Slower response than subscribers

10% of all EER sessions contribute to the Empatherapy Community Healing Fund

EER & The Healing Fund

10% of all EER sessions contribute to the Empatherapy Healing Fund. Healing Fund resources may be used to partially or fully subsidize EER sessions for those in hardship.

A Note on Safety

If you are at risk of harming yourself or others, or need immediate help, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line.

EER is not equipped for emergency intervention.

How to Request EER Support

Email hello@empatherapy.co with the subject line "EER Request"

Please Include:

  • Your name (first name is enough)
  • Whether you're a current EER subscriber or existing client
  • Brief description of what you need support with
  • How urgent this feels for you

You will never be asked to justify your need for support.

We aim to respond to all EER requests and schedule within 72 hours. Subscribers receive priority.

1:1 Empatherapy Sessions

When your emotions ask to be felt, not silenced

These sessions aren't quick fixes—they're sacred places.

Sessions, 1:1s, deeply intuited. Each session invites you into a cocoon of safety, slowing, and self-compassion. Whether you're actively grieving or just feel numb, overwhelmed, navigating heartbreak, identity shifts, or breakthrough—this is where your feelings have permission to unfold.

This is a sacred space to be fully held, and to come back home to yourself. Your emotions are welcomed—not fixed, analyzed, or interpreted as though they're problems. This is a space where presence becomes medicine, and your story is never too much.

Come to simply slow down. Come calm, relax, surrender—and remember.

What to Expect

Intuitive Counselling

Compassionate, non-directive presence that follows your emotional truth wherever it needs to go.

Somatic Practices

Intuitive movement, grounding, body awareness to help you reconnect to felt sense.

Energy Work

Reiki, chakra alignment, guided meditation, and visualization for deep nervous system support.

Creative Tools

Music, images, ritual, journaling, art, and cognitive-based supports as needed.

Reflective Listening

Narrative and reflective practices that help you hear yourself more clearly.

Integration Support

For emotional emergence, crises, loss, grief, or relational difficulties—affirm joy through grounded clarity.

My Approach

You lead, I follow. This isn't directive therapy.

Sessions are pure presence—intuitive listening from a place where every part of your truth might unfold or reveal itself—sometimes all at once through a single breath or in silent stillness.

There are no agendas, no performance expectations. You might speak. You might cry. You might laugh gently or rage or just sit in holy silence. Whatever unfolds is welcome. It's all medicine.

Sessions Are Recommended For:

  • Existential questions and life transitions
  • Grief, loss, and bereavement
  • Relationship difficulties or identity questioning
  • Creative blocks and nervous system dysregulation
  • Emotional overflow or spiritual emergence

Please note: Sessions are not crisis intervention, severe psychiatric crisis, or emergency replacement for medical diagnosis or psychiatric supervision. If you're currently in therapy, Empatherapy can support your process as complementary somatic and emotional work.

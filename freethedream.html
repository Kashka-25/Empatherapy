FreeTheDream

Heart-led, practical mentorship that turns your vision into a sustainable reality—without the burnout, confusion, or corporate bullshit.

You Have the Gift. Now Build the Structure.

You're a healer, a coach, a creative, or a guide. You have skill. You have heart. You know you're meant to do this work.

But somewhere between the vision and the execution, things get murky. The marketing feels gross. The systems overwhelm you. You're paralyzed by pricing, unclear on your offer, or exhausted from trying to do it all.

That's where I come in.

FreeTheDream is practical, empathetic mentorship for sole traders and ethical entrepreneurs who need to build the bones of their business—without losing their soul in the process.

What We Build Together

Clarify Your Offer

We distill your services into a clear, compelling offer. One sentence that tells people exactly what you do, who it's for, and why it matters.

Craft Your Message

Create authentic copy that feels true to you—no hype, no manipulation. Just honest words that attract your people.

Build Simple Systems

Set up the minimum viable infrastructure: a clean website or landing page, booking calendar, payment links, intake forms, and email.

Create Sustainable Rhythms

Design a content strategy that doesn't require constant posting. Show up in a way that honors your energy and nervous system.

Set Boundaries That Protect

Establish pricing, session structures, and delivery workflows that prevent burnout and honor your worth.

Take Consistent Action

Move from stuck to shipping. We break everything into clear next steps so you actually launch.

  • You're a creative or guide who struggles with business structure
  • You know what you want to offer but can't articulate it clearly
  • You're overwhelmed by tech, marketing, and systems
  • You want to build something sustainable without sacrificing your values

  • You need someone to walk beside you and make it real

What You'll Walk Away With:

  • Messaging that feels authentic and attracts your people
  • A simple, functional website or landing page
  • Working systems for booking, payments, and email
  • A content rhythm that doesn't burn you out
  • Pricing and boundaries that protect your energy
  • The confidence to actually launch

How It Works

Discovery Call (30 min — Free)

We meet. You share your vision, I assess where you're stuck. We decide if we're a fit.

Foundation Session (90 min — $300)

We clarify your offer, refine your message, and map out your simplest path to launch. You leave with a clear plan and next steps.

Build Phase (3–6 weeks — $1,200–$2,500)

This is where we execute. I guide you through building your website, setting up systems, creating copy, and preparing to launch. Includes weekly check-ins, async support, and implementation guidance.

Launch Support (Optional — $500)

Extra hand-holding during your first month live. Content strategy, troubleshooting, and refinement as you step into your new offer.

How to Connect

Contact Empatherapy

For general questions about our services, the Healing Fund, or to get guidance on which practitioner might be right for you:

Email: hello@empatherapy.co

What to include:

  • Your name
  • What you're seeking support with
  • Any questions you have

We respond within 24-48 hours.

Connect with a Specific Therapist

To book a discovery call or session with a specific practitioner:

Step 1: Click "Book Discovery Call" on their practitioner card

Step 2: Email opens with subject line pre-filled

Step 3: Include your name and brief note about what you're seeking

Step 4: They'll respond with availability for your free 10-minute discovery call

Example for Cassidy:

Subject: Discovery Call with Cassidy
Body: "Hi, I'm [name]. I'm interested in psychedelic integration support and would love to book a discovery call to see if we're a good fit."

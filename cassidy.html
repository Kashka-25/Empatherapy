A Space Where You Can Arrive Exactly As You Are

I created Empatherapy because I needed it to exist — and I suspect you did too.

I've lived inside the full spectrum of emotional experience: the overwhelm, the numbness, the searching, the strange and beautiful unravelling that comes when you stop pretending you're fine. I've sat with altered states, with grief, with the particular exhaustion of being someone who feels everything deeply in a world that moves too fast.

What I found, again and again, was that healing doesn't come from being fixed. It comes from being truly met — without judgment, without agenda, without someone trying to manage you into someone more convenient.

That's what I offer.

How I Work

My approach is intuitive, responsive, and deeply collaborative. I don't follow a script. I follow you — your energy, your language, what your body is communicating beneath the words, what your emotions are trying to say beneath the story.

Each session is a living conversation that moves through whatever layers are ready to be touched. Sometimes that's talking. Sometimes it's somatic tracking, visualization, or energy work. Sometimes it's sitting together in a silence that holds more than words can.

I draw from a wide range of practices without being dogmatic about any of them. I trust the moment more than any methodology.

Holistic Counselling

Conversation-led, body-aware, emotion-honoring. Sessions that meet you where you are and move with you, not ahead of you.

Somatic Awareness

Your body holds what your mind can't yet articulate. We work with physical sensation as a guide, not a problem to solve.

Shadow & IFS-Informed Work

Compassionate inquiry into the parts of yourself you've learned to hide or disown — met with curiosity, not judgment.

Energy Work & Reiki

Offered as a channel for clarity and settling — never as control. A supportive layer when words aren't enough.

Psychedelic Preparation & Integration

Grounded, ethical, non-clinical support before and after expanded-state experiences. Making meaning from what moved you.

Business & Vision Mentorship

For soul-led creatives and healers building something real — without burning out or selling out.
